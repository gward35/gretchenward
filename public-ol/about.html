Hi, I'm Gretchen and I'm interested in about a Jillion things.

Sci-fi, art, the universe, strawberry shortcake, sports. I love design, code, and drawing. I'm always learning and I love when I figure out something new or come up with a solution, especially one I didn't see coming. It ends up with me ripping out my curls (a joke) or me smiling and saying "Ohhhhhhhh".

With HTML, CSS, PHP, JS and jQuery I can implement my designs on the web that are intuitive clean and performant. I want to accomplish way more than what you see on this site and I want to be able to do it while juggling and reading a comic simultaneously. I'm currently learning several JS libraries including Angular and D3.js I'm also tinkering with hardware using Node.js Arduino and Raspberry Pi. If you have any questions about me or my work drop me a line.
