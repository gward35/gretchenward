Designing Our Future


Web Design | Web Development


Designing Our Future: Sustainable Landscapes is a collection of 40 cases studies featuring the some of the most successfull and sustainable landscape architecture projects in the world. The original site was static. I re-built the entire site to make it responsive. One of the more challenging and fun tasks of this site was figuring out the best design and interaction pertaining to the navigation, specifically on mobile devices. With so many case studies I went with a custom drawer menu that displays case studies, animations, and education entries, their sub regions (if applicable), and finally their main content. The old site relied heavily on a very outdated version of jQuery tools for the landing page slider, I implemented Slick to make things simpler and more up to date.
